⚔️ How Every Event Is Played

Every event is head-to-head Round robin

Each family plays every other family once. Count each family's wins in that event, then rank them 1st–4th to award points. This is the same for all 8 events.

The 6 matchups (played for every event):

Most wins = 1st-place points. Fewest wins = last. (Every event = 6 matches.)

🤝 Fair Play Rule

When family sizes don't match (like Three-Legged Race or Volleyball), each side uses the same number of players — match the smaller family. Extra players sub in and cheer! 📣

🎮 The 8 Events

🏐 Volleyball H2H

First team to 10 points wins the match. Play all 6 matchups. Most wins ranks 1st.

🏸 Badminton H2H

First to 10 points wins the match. Play all 6 matchups. Most wins ranks 1st.

🤺 Joust H2H

2 players per family. In each matchup the two families' jousters go one-on-one on the balls — knock the other off. Family that wins the duel(s) wins the match. Play all 6 matchups; most wins ranks 1st.

🥤 Suck It Up H2H

2 players per family. Straw only — move Skittles from plate to plate in 60 seconds. In each matchup, the family that moves more Skittles wins. Play all 6 matchups; most wins ranks 1st.

🌀 Twister H2H

One player per family. In each matchup the two players share the mat — last one standing wins. Play all 6 matchups; most wins ranks 1st.

🥏 Frisbee Toss H2H

One thrower per family. In each matchup, the longer in-bounds throw wins (or best of 3 target hits). Play all 6 matchups; most wins ranks 1st.

🛞 Wheelbarrow Race H2H

2 players per family — one walks on their hands while their partner holds their ankles. In each matchup, the two families' pairs race side-by-side to the finish line; first pair across wins the match. Swap roles and re-run if a pair drops. Play all 6 matchups; most wins ranks 1st.

🦵 Three-Legged Race H2H

2 players per family, legs tied together. In each matchup, the two families' pairs race side-by-side to the finish line — first pair across wins the match. Play all 6 matchups; most wins ranks 1st.

⚖️ Tiebreakers

Tie inside an event (families with the same wins): those families play one more head-to-head round to settle it.

Tie for the trophy (same total points at the end): tied families face off in a sudden-death Three-Legged Race for the win. 🦵🏆
